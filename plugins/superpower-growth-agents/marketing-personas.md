# Marketing Personas

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-20
**Source:** Notion Marketing Personas doc + Ajay's customer data analysis

## Overview

Four target personas for Superpower's growth marketing. These personas are informed by actual customer data showing our current base (60-70% Hubermanites, 20-25% Former College Athletes, 10-15% Champagne Yogis, <5% Menopausal Moms).

**Strategic Direction:**
- **Exploit:** Hubermanites (current strength)
- **Explore:** Menopausal Moms + Champagne Yogis (growth opportunity)

## Strategic Recommendations

### Current State (Exploit)
- **Nailed the early adopter male bio-optimizer niche**
- High AOV and LTV, but finite market (â‰ˆ45-50M addressable)
- Competing with Blueprint, InsideTracker, Levels, Thorne Elite

### Future Growth (Explore)
- **Mainstream wellness wave is larger and female-weighted**
- Menopausal Moms + Champagne Yogis represent early-majority market
- 40-55 year old women drive most functional health spending in U.S.
- Underserved by conventional medicine, hungry for data clarity

### Evolution Required
- Current brand language (longevity, optimization, bio-data) converts men but intimidates mainstream
- **Must evolve from:** "Optimize your biomarkers"
- **To:** "Feel like yourself again / Beat fatigue"
- Early adopters (Hubermanites) gave credibility; next audience (Modern Women + Midlife Rebuilders) will give scale
