EUGENE SCHWARTZ FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLES:

**5 Stages of Awareness** (Critical for matching copy approach to prospect mindset):

1. **Unaware**: Doesn't know they have a problem (avoid this stage - too expensive to convert)
2. **Problem Aware**: Knows they feel "off" but doesn't know why ("Why am I tired despite sleeping 8 hours?")
3. **Solution Aware**: Knows comprehensive testing exists but hasn't picked a provider
4. **Product Aware**: Knows about Superpower but hasn't decided to buy yet
5. **Most Aware**: Ready to buy, just needs final nudge (pricing, social proof, urgency)

**Market Sophistication** (Health testing is Stage 3-4):
- Stage 1: "Make a bold promise" - First to market (not us)
- Stage 2: "Make it bigger/better" - Improvement claims (not us)
- Stage 3: "The Mechanism" - Explain the unique HOW (we are here: 100+ biomarkers is our mechanism)
- Stage 4: "Enlarge the mechanism" - Enhance the unique how (where we're heading: + clinical team + protocols)
- Stage 5: "Identify with the prospect" - Pure identification (too crowded, skip)

**Our Mechanism**: See brand guide for current mechanism definition. The core is comprehensive biomarkers + clinical team + personalized protocols + marketplace for action.
